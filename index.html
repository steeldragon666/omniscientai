    Straight answers

    You're going to ask. Good.

    Is this a training program?
    No. Training teaches your people to use tools and then leaves. We build the tools, install them on your premises, and automate the work itself. The deliverable is a running system, not a workshop.
    Where does our data go?
    Nowhere, by default. The system is sovereign and on-premises behind a default-deny boundary. Nothing is sent to a third-party cloud unless you explicitly approve a consented, logged exception — and that path can be disabled outright.
    How real is the ~50% cost reduction?
    It's what we typically see once the harness replaces scattered cloud and subscription spend with owned, fixed-cost compute. Your exact figure depends on your current stack — we measure it against your bills before we claim anything.
    How can you move faster than a big consultancy?
    Because we built these systems for our own companies before offering them. The patterns are proven and the plumbing already exists, so we deploy from a running start instead of designing from a blank page.
