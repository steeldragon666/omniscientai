Privacy Policy

omniscientai.io · Last updated 7 July 2026

This policy explains how Omniscient AI ("we", "us") handles personal information collected through this website, in line with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs). It reflects a simple stance we also build into our products: collect the minimum, keep it in Australia where practicable, and be able to show our workings.

1. What we collect

  • Enquiry details you give us — name, company, work email address and the content of your message, when you submit the enquiry form or email us.
  • Correspondence — emails you exchange with us.
  • Technical logs — our website is a static site; standard, short-lived access logs (IP address, user agent, pages requested) are generated by our hosting provider for security and availability purposes.

We do not run advertising trackers on this website, and we do not buy, sell or trade personal information.

2. Why we collect it

  • To respond to your enquiry and, where relevant, arrange a briefing or proposal.
  • To administer and secure the website.
  • To keep records reasonably required for business and legal purposes.

We only send you marketing material if you ask for it, and every such message includes a working way to opt out.

3. How enquiry data is processed

Enquiry form submissions are delivered to us by a third-party form-processing provider before arriving in our Australian-hosted mailbox. Form processors may store submissions on infrastructure outside Australia (typically the United States); by submitting the form you consent to that transmission. If you prefer not to use the form, email us directly at enquiries@omniscientai.io.

4. Storage, security and retention

We take reasonable steps to protect personal information from misuse, interference, loss and unauthorised access — including access controls and encryption in transit. We keep enquiry records only as long as needed for the purposes above, then delete or de-identify them.

5. Disclosure

We do not disclose your personal information to third parties except: our service providers strictly for the purposes above (hosting, form processing, email); where you ask us to; or where required by law. We never use client or enquirer information to train AI models.

6. Access, correction and complaints

You may request access to, or correction of, the personal information we hold about you by emailing enquiries@omniscientai.io. We'll respond within a reasonable period. If you believe we have breached the APPs and are unsatisfied with our response, you can complain to the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at oaic.gov.au.

7. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time; the current version will always be published at this address with its date.
